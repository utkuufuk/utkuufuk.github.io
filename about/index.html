About

Hi there, my name is Utku. I live in Turkey and work remotely as a software engineer at Portchain.

Through several years of professional experience and countless side-projects, I’ve been exposed to a wide variety of domains such as image processing, GPGPU programming, machine learning, frontend/backend web development, web scraping and cryptography.

I have become quite the generalist as a result of having worked on a lot of different kinds of projects and organizations (think national space agency and small startup). That’s why my blog might contain posts with pretty unrelated topics. But hey, I promise it’ll always be (mostly) about “programming” :)

If you like my articles, you might want to subscribe to get email updates on my future posts every once in a while.
